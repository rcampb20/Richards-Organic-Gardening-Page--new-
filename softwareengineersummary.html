Software Engineer Summary

What I have always wanted to be

I have a passion for learning new tasks and have always gravitated towards challenges that stimulate the mind. Since childhood, I have wanted to be an engineer due to its nature of problem-solving and the possibility of creativity. Although it is not the equivalent of the electronics field that I learned during my four years in high school that sparked my interest. I cannot think of another field in modern history that impacts so many different aspects of everyday life. The knowledge from software engineering will help not only in my personal career development but also in my personal life. Understanding, then excelling in the building blocks of how the system works, will be priceless. As my grandmother used to say, “easy come, easy go”. I am not experienced in this field, but I am driven to gain the knowledge to succeed regardless of the challenges. Planning and preparation are the key along with a lot of patience. Gardening, as well as in many other tasks in life, have shown me that it will make the process a bit easier by doing research. During my journey of gardening, there have been a few people that deserved credit, one of them being John Kohler. I have gained a wealth of knowledge by watching his You Tube Channel. I will approach coding as I do with other things in life which will hopefully bring me success.
